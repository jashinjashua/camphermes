Welcome to Camp Hermes

Where Men Are Forged

Luffy, Naruto, Goku, Baki — all of them went through intense training camps to level up and become stronger. Camp Hermes is designed for brutal men who want to push their limits. Surrounded by stunning nature, this camp offers top-tier training, the finest quality meat, and an unforgettable experience alongside other like-minded men.

Our goal at Camp Hermes is to take your body to its highest potential. We focus on coordination, strength, explosive power, balance, and — most importantly — willpower. Our mission is simple: to evolve you into your most brutal and powerful form.

Camp Hermes is not for everyone. But if you are ready to become a brutal man, welcome home.

— Hermes / God of Athletes and Sports
